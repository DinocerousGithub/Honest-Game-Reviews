Super Mario Odyssey

Super Mario Odyssey is a refreshing change of pace from most games within the Super Mario franchise. With its stunning visuals and addictive game play, this collect-a-thon is sure to please any gamer that has played a Mario game. Moreover, the game has constant challenges to overcome in Luigi's Balloon World mode. Overall, this game recieves a:

Rating: 4.8/5


The Legend Of Zelda: Breath of The Wild

Breath of the Wild is a long and ardueous journey, however it is a good one. It's a nice departure from the mainline games, removing many things such as heart pieces and unlimited durability and stamina - but still rewards the player with spirit orbs that can contribute to a full heart container or stamina vessel. Completing the shrines that award these spirit orbs felt like an accomplishment, not a nuisance. Moreover, the game gives the player more freedom than any game before it. You can complete any dungeons you want, and then go to Ganon (main boss of game). Overall, this game recieves a:

Rating: 5/5
