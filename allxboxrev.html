Cuphead

Cuphead is an amazingly difficult game. I personally could not get past the tutorial (ITS A JOKE PEOPLE). With its great 30's art style, all hand drawn, you would think this game would be easy. In spite of this, regardless game will kick your butt. I have died so many times in this game, but it always felt fair. However, There are some some terrible Random Number Generated (RNG) sequences within the game. Overall, this game recieves a:

Rating: 4.2/5
